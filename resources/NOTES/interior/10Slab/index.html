    10Slab

    Updated Date: JUN 23, 2021

    explanation

    1. slab: convergent boudary
      trench (20-30 km seismogenic faults; deepest event ~700 km); volcanism; accretionary prism (俯冲板块下潜时被上盘板块刮削下来的沉积盖层和洋壳碎片，连同原地深海沉积物堆积到海沟的向陆侧而成).
      why subduction: geoid anomaly
    2. types of dip angle: Chilean type (arc under compression, lower angle, large magnitude earthquake); Marianas type (arc under extension, larger angle, small magnitude earthquake)
    3. oldest oceanic crust: 150 Ma, distance from mid-ocean ridge to trench is 2500 km (Pacific), with expansion velocity 5 cm/yr, maximum at 13 cm/yr, rising velocity of the plume head is 2 m/yr
    4. subduction layering (100 km):
    5. mantle lithosphere: 170 Ma subduction initiation; 100 Ma is required for the slab reaching trench
    6. whether the subduction could happen ?
      It depends on the crustal density and crustal thickness.
      for MORB, little pressure results in large density, even larger than that of pyrolite. Approaching to the 660-km, its density is lower than that of pyrolite.
      subduction condition: for all oceanic crust; density difference should be more than 0.1 g/cm3; continental crust thickness should be less than 15 km, 30 km for oceanic crust thickness.
    7. subduction of the continental crust: from SiO2 to coesite (柯石英) to stishovite (司石英)
      evidence of the deep subduction: coesite, stishovite and diamond exist in the surface, but they only formed in the high pressure environment, which indicates a deep original depth. So there exists deep subduction and rollback.
      rollback mechanism: denser material sinks, slab fractures after the accretion. The broken part sinks as its density difference is getting lower, so is starts roolbacks upward.
    8. oceanic subduction: hydrothermal facilitates the increase of the volatiles; mid-ocean ridge metamorphism generates serpentinite due to HTHP; serpentinite weakens the strength of lithosphere, which facilitates the bending of the plate, meanwhile the dehydration causes deep seismicity; 由于蛇纹石脱水导致的双震区(下层发生地震与蛇纹石化有关)-双震：浅层断裂多，易蛇纹石化（含水）；深层矿物脱水-深震
    9. evidence of the sediment subduction: 10Be in the extrusive lava, 10Be mainly concentrates in the oceanic sediments and DO NOT form in the mantle, but has been found in the Earth's interior (250 km)
    10. thermal structure of the subduction zone: heat flow increases along the subduction direction
      water \(\rightarrow\) partial melt \(\rightarrow\) advection
      410-km phase change exothermic, facilitates the subduction; 660-km phase change endothermic, prohibits the subduction
    11. subduction mineral
      dehydration increases with the depth; melting point decreases with the depth; 火山弧下方的一系列低频地震是由于地幔底辟的深部岩浆活动产生
    12. origin of the deep seismicity: transformational faulting of metastable olivine; dehydration embrittlement; thermal runaway
    13. characteristics of the slab earthquake: older slab gives rise to deeper, meanwhile smaller magnitude seismicity; subduction shallower than 5000 km exists no deep seismicity; older slab needs more time to heat, with deeper.
    14. why there exists no deep seismicity under 700 km ?
      Because dehydration occurs above 300-km depth; metastable phase change occurs within 500-600 km; thermal runaway occurs at 700 km. Due to the viscosity, deep seismicity will not occur at over 700 km depth.
      火山弧下方的一系列低频地震是由于地幔底辟的深部岩浆活动产生。在slab上两个平行带，上面是沿逆断层的正应力，下层是拉应力，下层地震发生可能与蛇纹石化有关。
