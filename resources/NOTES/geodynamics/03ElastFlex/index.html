    Elasiticity & Flexure

    terminology

    flexure: the action of bending or curving, or the condition of being bent or curved

    unit conversion & typical constants

    1. tectonic stress: 10 Mpa (1 pa = 1 N/m2; 1 Mpa = 1e6 pa)
    2. Young's Modulus for typical rocks: 70 Gpa
    3. magnitude for strain in the lithosphere: 1e-4
    4. Possion's ratio: 0.25
    5. density: 3000 SI

    elastic relation between stress & strain

    1. Lame description
    2. Young's Modulus & Possion's ratio description
      \(\(\sigma_1=(\lambda+2\mu)\epsilon_1+\lambda\epsilon_2+\lambda\epsilon_3\\ ......\\ \epsilon_1=\frac{\sigma_1}{E}-\frac{\nu \sigma_2}{E}-\frac{\nu \sigma_3}{E}\)\)

    uniaxial stress

    \(\(\sigma_2=\sigma_3=0\\ \epsilon_1=\frac{\sigma_1}{E}\\ \epsilon_2=\epsilon_3=-\frac{\nu \sigma_1}{E}\)\) 1. deformation of elastic plate under its own gravity \(\(\sigma_1=\rho g(l-y)\\ \epsilon1=\sigma_1/E\\ \Delta h=\int_0^l{\epsilon_1 dy}=\frac{\rho g l^2}{2E}\)\) 2. Derive the force magnitude for slab pull and ridge push 3. Derive the Stokes velocity for a rising diapir

    uniaxial strain

    for sedimentary basins:
    \(\(\epsilon_2=\epsilon_3=0\\ \sigma_1=(\lambda+2\mu)\epsilon_1\\ \sigma_2=\sigma_3=\lambda \epsilon_1=\frac{\lambda \sigma_1}{\lambda+2\mu}\)\) 1. Stress state in a sedimentary basin 2. Deviatoric stresses in the basin
    \(\(p=\frac{\sigma_1+\sigma_2+\sigma_3}{3}\\ \tau_i=\sigma_i-p\)\) 3. Surface stress change due to erosion
    \(\(\sigma_1=\sigma_2=\sigma_3=\rho gh\\ \bar{\sigma_1}=0\\ \Delta \sigma_1 = \bar{\sigma_1}-\sigma_1\\ \Delta \sigma_2=\Delta \sigma_3=(\frac{\nu}{1-\nu})\Delta \sigma_1\)\)

    elastic bending or flexure of 2D plates

    concerns the bending of a elastic plate under applied load
    define flexural rigidity (挠曲刚度): \(D=\frac{Eh^3}{12(1-\nu^2)}\)
