    Plate Tectonics

    The World Atlas names seven major plates:

    1. African
    2. Antarctic
    3. Eurasian
    4. Indo-Australian
    5. North American
    6. Pacific
    7. South American.

    How to define a plate vertically & horizontally?

    1. Tectonic plates are gigantic pieces of the Earth's crust and uppermost mantle, i.e. pieces of the lithosphere.
    2. The lithosphere, which is the rigid outermost shell of a planet (the crust and upper mantle), is broken into tectonic plates.

    The types of plate boundaries

    1. convergent
    2. divergent
    3. transform

    what is Wilson Cycle ?

    The Wilson Cycle is a model that describes the opening and closing of ocean basins and the subduction and divergence of tectonic plates during the assembly and disassembly of supercontinents.

    Phases of Wilson cycle: From ten o'clock position clockwise: (10) initial pre-drift extension, (12) rift-to-drift phase, initial opening of an oceanic basin, (2 and 4) seafloor spreading, widening of the basin, (6) subduction of oceanic lithosphere, closure of the basin, (8) continent-continent collision
